Moving on from online to offline school

Moving on from online to offline school isn't easy we as students face many problems that we have to overcome.
Many of us have to adapt to new sleep schedules and new routines overall. We have to reach school on time, we have to wear masks most of the day
and its more tiring. Alongside these challenges there are less significant ones too, For example carrying a heavy bag all day , we cannot leave stuff
at school (like we normally did before the pandemic) because there is no certainty when the school might shut down. There are ways to overcome
these challenges and thats what me and fellow team mates(Amarthya Gupta and Aryan K , For more information read the 'About' page) have been doing.
We have chosen the problem of adjusting to our new sleep schedules, and we have come up with a feasible solution for this . Check the 'Solution' page
for more information.
