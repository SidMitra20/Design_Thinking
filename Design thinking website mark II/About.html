About this project

Why are we doing this project? This is part of our Design thinking project, we are given with a problem and we have to come up with
a unique solution. We interviewed our friends and learned about their problems during this sudden transition between online to offline
school . Some of our friends found it difficult to adapt to a new sleep schedule , some of them stated that carrying the bag around is difficult
and we cannot leave our books at school because of uncertainty . Me and my group mates on this project wanted to find a solution for the sleep schedule problem.

    • Planning process

    1.What problem do we work on- We decided to work on the problem of sleep schedule

    2. We had to break down the problem into smaller parts and we brainstormed ideas.

    3. We had to come up with a solution , we did a lot of research on ways to fall asleep quickly and how to wake up on time.

    4. We had to present our solution in an appealing manner , we decided to make a video with images and us narrating.

    5. Our solution can benefit anyone trying to adapt to a new sleep schedule , We decided to make
    a website where we inform people about our solution and project.
